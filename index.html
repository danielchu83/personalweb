Daniel Chu / AI systems and product notes

I build AI systems for the messy middle of real work.

Partner Group Product Manager at Microsoft, leading M365 Copilot Mobile product work and building multi-agent systems that turn product intent into reviewable, testable workflow.

At work and at home, I build these systems to understand what breaks when agents touch files, tools, memory, and decisions that need review.

This site is the notebook around that work: practical notes on evals, review loops, recovery paths, and the product judgment that shows up after the impressive first answer.
