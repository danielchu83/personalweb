Life / reflection

Notes on learning in seasons

Some weeks are for collecting fragments. Some are for making the shape visible.

Some weeks I collect fragments: a paragraph from a book, a strange product detail, a bug I do not understand yet, a conversation that keeps echoing later. None of it looks like progress in the moment.

Other weeks are for making the shape visible. I cut the examples down, group the ones that belong together, and finally see what I was circling.

I am trying not to force both modes to happen at the same time. Collecting and shaping ask for different kinds of attention.
