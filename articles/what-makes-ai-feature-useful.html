What makes an AI feature feel useful?

A working list: speed, control, reversibility, clear failure modes, and enough context to avoid making the user babysit it.

Draft note. To me, a useful AI feature saves time without making the user babysit it. It is fast, reversible, clear about failure, and honest about what it does not know.
